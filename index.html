Research

My research interests include computer vision, machine learning, robotics, and neuroscience. I envision a machine that can dynamically adapt and evolve to the changing environment similar to our brain. Current static neural networks call for the scale-up of their model size to compensate for the lack of adaptability, and make the models hard to be deployed in changing unstructured environments. To address this issue, I am interested in dynamic neural networks that can make machines flexibly learn where to focus and evolve accordingly, like human brain.
